What you'll need to send your claim

You'll need to tell us the:

  • dates that you travelled to or from work
  • name and email address of a workplace contact who we can ask to confirm each journey you're claiming for
  • details of the person or company who needs paying back, including bank details if we've not paid them before
{{ govukInsetText({ text: "The person or company who needs paying back could be yourself, a family member or friend, your employer or a transport company." }) }}

If you're claiming for travel by taxi

You'll need to:

  • tell us how many journeys you made each day
  • tell us the total cost of your work travel you're claiming for
  • send us an invoice or receipt for any journeys you're claiming for

You can either send us:

  • a receipt or invoice that contains all the journeys you're claiming for
  • separate receipts and invoices for each single journey

If you're claiming for lifts from a friend, colleague or family member

You'll need to tell us the total mileage travelled or the total number of journeys on each date.
