What you'll need to make a claim

You'll need:

  • the dates of each journey you took during work
  • the postcode of the place where each journey started and finished
  • the total cost of each journey you're claiming for
  • details of the person or company we need to pay, including bank details if we've not paid them before
{{ govukInsetText({ text: "The person or company we need to pay could be yourself, a family member or friend, your employer or a transport company." }) }}

You'll need to send us one of the following:

  • a receipt or invoice that contains all the journeys you're claiming for
  • separate receipts and invoices for each journey

If you're an employee'

You'll need the name and email address of a workplace contact we can ask to confirm each journey you're claiming for. This needs to be someone like your line manager or HR manager.
