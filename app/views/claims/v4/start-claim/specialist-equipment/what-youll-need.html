What you'll need to make a claim

You'll need to tell us:

  • the date you bought the equipment - you can find this on the receipt or invoice
  • the person or company we need to pay - this can be you, a family member, friend, employer or anyone who paid for your equipment
  • the name, address, email and bank details of any new person or company we haven't paid before

You can claim for more than one piece of specialist equipment.

How to confirm your claim

You'll need to send us an invoice or receipt for the equipment you are claiming for.
