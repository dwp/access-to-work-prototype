Prototypes

This page documents the prototypes which form the Access to Work (AtW) service.

AtWIS prototype

The AtWIS prototype is the part of the service which is used by agents to manage cases, applications and documents.

View AtWIS prototype

Apply for AtW prototype

The AtW apply prototype covers the citizen facing side of the service which allows citizens to check their eligibility and start & complete an application.

View Apply prototype

Claims prototype

The AtW claims prototype covers the citizen facing side of the service which allows citizens to claim back the money given to them as part of the grant.
