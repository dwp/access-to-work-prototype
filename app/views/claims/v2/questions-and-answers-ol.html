GOV.UK One Login questions and answers

What is GOV.UK One Login?

GOV.UK One Login lets you sign in to government services using the same email address and password. It is not the same as Government Gateway.

In future you’ll need to use it to access all services on GOV.UK.

What do I need to create a GOV.UK One Login?

You'll need:

  • an email address
  • a way to get security codes – either a mobile phone number or an authenticator app

To prove your identity, you’ll need:

  • a valid photo ID, such as a passport or driving licence

Or, if you haven’t got photo ID, you can still prove your identity with:

  • your bank or building society account details
  • your National Insurance number

How long does it take?

Creating a GOV.UK One Login takes a few minutes.

Do I need to do anything before 26 November 2025?

You cannot sign in to Access to Work with GOV.UK One Login before 26 November, but you can create a GOV.UK One Login account if you do not already have one.

The first time you sign in with GOV.UK One Login, you’ll also be asked to add your first name and last name to complete your user profile.

I already have a GOV.UK One Login account. Can I use it for Access to Work?

Yes. If you already use GOV.UK One Login for another government service, you can use it to sign into Access to Work from 26 November 2025.

Should I use the same email address as my current Access to Work account?

Using the same email address for GOV.UK One Login will make it simpler, but it’s not essential.

I’m having problems signing up to GOV.UK Login. What should I do?

Ensure your email, password and security code have been entered correctly.

If the problems continue, contact GOV.UK One Login.
