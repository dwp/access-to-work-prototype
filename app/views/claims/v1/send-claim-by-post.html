If you cannot claim online, you can send Access to Work claims by post.

You'll need to:

  • fill in the paper claim form we sent with your Access to Work award letter
  • include any receipts or invoices we ask for in the claims form

You can contact Access to Work to get a paper claim form if you need one.

You should post your claim to Access to Work:

Access to Work Service Centre
Harrow Jobcentre
Mail Handling Site A
Wolverhampton
WV98 1JE

You'll get a text message or email to let you know when we've received your claim form.

Get help

You can contact us for free by:

  • Telephone: 0800 121 7479
  • Textphone: 0800 121 7579
  • Relay UK (opens in new tab), (if you cannot hear or speak on the phone): 18001 then 0800 121 7479
  • Using the Video Relay Service (opens in new tab) for British Sign Language – find out how to use the Video Relay Service (opens in new tab).

Opening times: Monday to Friday, 9am to 5pm (except public holidays)
