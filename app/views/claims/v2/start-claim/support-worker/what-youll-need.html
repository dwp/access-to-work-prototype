What you'll need to make a claim

You'll need to send a claim for each type of support you get. For example, if you use a British Sign Language interpretor and have a driver, you'll need to send 2 claims.

If different people or companies paid for your support, you'll also need to send separate claims for each.

You'll need:

  • the dates you had support
  • to tell us how many hours of support you had on each date
  • the name and email address of the person or company who we can ask to confirm the support you're claiming for
  • the details of the person or company who needs paying, including bank details if we've not paid them before
{{ govukInsetText({ text: "The person or company who needs paying back could be anyone who paid for your support worker costs. This includes yourself, a friend, a family member, or your employer." }) }}

You'll need to send us an invoice or receipt for any support worker costs you're claiming for.

If you are self-employed or owrk at the same organisation as the person providing the support

You'll need to send us a digital copy of a signed timesheet or payslip for the person who provided your support.
