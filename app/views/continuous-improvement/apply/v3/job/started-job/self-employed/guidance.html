Information you'll need - self-employed

What you'll need to enter in this application

Your Unique Taxpayer Reference (opens in new tab) if you started trading before 1 April.

What you'll need to provide when a case manager contacts you

Your Unique Taxpayer Reference if you did not include it in your application.

If you've been trading for more than a year, we'll ask for your accounts or tax returns that show an annual turnover of at least £6,500.

If you've been trading for less than a year, we'll ask for your business plan and 3-year financial forecast to show that you expect a turnover of £6,500.
