What you'll need to make a claim

You'll need to:

  • tell us the date on the invoice
  • send us an invoice or receipt that shows the cost of the adaptation
  • tell us the details of the person or company we need to pay,including their bank details if we've not paid them before

You can claim for more than one adaptation in a single claim if the work was carried out by the same person or company.
