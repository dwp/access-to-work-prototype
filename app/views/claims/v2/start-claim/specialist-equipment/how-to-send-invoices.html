How to send us copies of receipts or invoices

We accept either:

  • photos or scans of paper receipts or invoices
  • digital receipts or invoices

We accept either:

  • the cost of the equipment
  • the date the equipment was bought
  • the full name of the person or company who provided the equipment
  • a short description of the equipment

Taking photos of receipts or invoices

Make sure:

  • you're in a well-lit place
  • you include the whole receipt or invoice in the image
  • you take the photo from above and not at an angle
  • the photo is not blurry

Scanning receipts or invoices

Make sure:

  • you include the whole receipt or invoice in the scan
  • the scan is not blurry
