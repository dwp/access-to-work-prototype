Sorry, we could not sign you in to Access to Work

If you successfully proved your identity with GOV.UK One Login, the details we have for you in Access to Work might be wrong. If your details have changed since you applied, contact Access to Work

If you cannot prove your identity with GOV.UK One Login, you can claim by post.

Claiming by post

You'll need to:

  • fill in the paper claim form we sent with your Access to Work award letter
  • include any receipts or invoices we ask for in the claims form

You can contact Access to Work to get a paper claim form if you need one.

Post your claim to:

Access to Work Service Centre
Harrow Jobcentre
Mail Handling Site A
Wolverhampton
WV98 1JE

You'll get a text message or email to let you know when we've received your claim form.
