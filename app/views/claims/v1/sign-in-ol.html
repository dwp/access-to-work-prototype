Sign in with GOV.UK One Login

You need to sign in with your GOV.UK One Login. If you do not have a GOV.UK One Login, you'll need to create one.

Prove your identity

To help keep your information safe, you may be asked to prove your identity.

You’ll need:

  • a valid photo ID, such as a passport or driving licence

Or, if you haven’t got photo ID, you can still prove your identity with:

  • your bank or building society account details
  • your National Insurance number
