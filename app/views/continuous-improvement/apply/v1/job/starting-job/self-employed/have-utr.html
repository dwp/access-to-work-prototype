Unique Taxpayer Reference (UTR)

A Unique Taxpayer Reference is a 10-digit number, for example, 1234567890. It will be on tax returns and other letters about tax. It may be called a ‘tax reference’, ‘UTR’ or appear next to the heading ‘official use’.
